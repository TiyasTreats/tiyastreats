Tiya's Treats Bakery Policies

Advance orders:orders must be made a day before treats are needed. For orders over 16 cookies, please give a week's notice.

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your order, ensuring your treats are fresh and ready!

Payment methods: cash, pay to cell, Orange Money.

Delivery in Gaborone is P40, and for UB and BAC students, it's only P20 on campus. Pickup is available with a quick communication a day before!
