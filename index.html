About Us

At Tiya’s Treats, every bite is a hug in cookie form. Founded by Tiya, a lifelong cookie enthusiast with a passion for turning simple ingredients into unforgettable moments, our bakery is all about spreading joy—one golden, chocolate-studded treat at a time. From classic chocolate chip to inventive flavor twists, each cookie is baked with love, and a sprinkle of nostalgia. Whether you’re celebrating, gifting, or just indulging yourself (no judgment here), Tiya’s Treats is here to make life a little sweeter.
